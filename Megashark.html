Megashark

Hardmode; ranged weapon

The Megashark is a fast firing, highly accurate minigun with a 50% chance to not consume ammo on use.

It is highly sought after due to it's substancial DPS and how fast it can be made after the Wall of flesh falls.

Ingredients:

  • The Mini Shark (purchased from the arms dealer)
  • Illegal gun parts
  • 5 shark fins
  • 20 souls of might

Steps:

  1. Connect the gun parts to the inner workings through the Mini Shark's flesh, do not worry about the cuts made in the process.
  2. Stuff it with the souls of might the same way you would do with ammo.
  3. Dice the fins and use them to fill the cuts and holes made in the first step.
  4. Leave the shark to rest in a salt water container, it is important that the container is at least 50% larger than the Mini Shark in all directions, this is to allow for the expeted growth.

    (you may also place the shark in a permeable bag large enough and hang it in the sea, so long as the bag is entirely within the water)

  5. After six hours or so, the Megashark should be completely formed and ready for use.
