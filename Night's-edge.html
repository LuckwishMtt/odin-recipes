Night's Edge

Pre-hardmode; post-skeletron; melee weapon

The Night's Edge is a broadsword capable of slashing through an area larger than it's own blade, creating afterimages in that range.

It was once considered the strongest of all swords; nowadays, however, it is simply the strongest sword one can aquire before falling the Wall of Flesh.

Ingredients:

  • 3 vines
  • 15 jungle spores
  • 12 stingers
  • 20 obsidian
  • 60 hellstones
  • 30 demonite (or crimtane) ores
  • The Muramasa (obtainable from the dungeon)

Steps:

  1. Mix the vines, jungle spores and stingers, then shape then in a metal anvil to make the Blade of Grass.
  2. Melt the obsidian and hellstones in a hellforge to make 20 hellstone bars, then, at an anvil, forge the Volcano.
  3. Melt your evil ore of choice at a normal furnace, and forge either the Light's Bane or the Blood Butcherer.
  4. With the Muramasa, Blade of Grass, Volcano, and either Light's Bane or Blood Butcherer at hand, bring them to an altar and let them form into the Night's Edge, now yours to wield.
